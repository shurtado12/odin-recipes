New York Strip

Description

This recipe will make an omelette that is soft and thin. The amount of eggs used will depend on the desired size of the omelette.

Ingredients

  • Carton of eggs
  • Unsalted butter
  • Himalayan Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cream Cheese
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend

Steps

  1. crack open your eggs into a bowl, being careful not to allow shells to spill in.
  2. Remove 2 tbsp of butter from the fridge and allow to sit at room temperature while the remaining steps are performed.
  3. With a fork or whisk, beat the eggs until a uniform consistency is formed.
  4. Add salt and Pepper to the beaten eggs.
  5. Turn the heat up on the stove to a low medium
  6. Put the butter on the skillet or pan and spread it so that it forms an even coat.
  7. Pour the omelette into the pan and cover.
  8. Allow to cook until the bottom of the omelette begins to solidify
  9. Flip the omelette over
  10. Add a scoop of cream cheese on the omelette.
  11. Allow to cook until the omelette starts to firm up
  12. Fold the omelette over on itself, so as to cover the cream cheese.
  13. Top with shredded cheese.

New York Strip

Description

This recipe is to make a delicious New York Strip. Cooking time will vary depending on the desired wellness.

Ingredients

  • Short Loin Strip Steak
  • Garlic
  • Unsalted butter
  • Himalayan Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Remove steak from the refrigerator and allow it to rest at room temperature between 15 and 20 minute
  2. Cut a 2 tablespoons of butter and allow to sit at room temperature while the other steps are taken.
  3. Season the steak heavily with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Turn the heat of your griddle or stove up between medium-high and high.
  5. Put the butter on the skillet or pan and spread it so that it forms an even coat.
  6. Add the rosemary and garlic to the griddle.
  7. Place steak on the skillet or grill and allow it to sit for 2 minutes. Try not to disturb the steak until it is time to flip it over.
  8. while it is cooking, pour some of the liquid contents that are in the pan over the steak.
  9. Turn the steak over and allow to cook for two more minutes.
  10. Continue pouring the liquid contents in the pan or griddle on the steak.
  11. Remove the steak from the heat and allow to rest for an additional 10 minutes at room temperature.
  12. Serve with your favorite sides such as mashed potatoes or macaroni and cheese.

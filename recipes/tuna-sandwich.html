Tuna Sandwich Recipe

Description

This is a personal reciped that will integrate some of the best aspects of tuna sandwiches which I have had over the years. This recipes is going to be based on different tuna sandwhiches into what I would consider the best tuna sandwich. You can feel free to add, subtract, or replace any aspect of this recipe to suit your own taste.

Ingredients

  • Can of Chunk Light Tuna
  • Cuban Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Kosher Dill Pickle Spears
  • Black Pepper
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Swiss Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the cuban bread into top and bottom slices and place it into a toaster oven. Toast for 6 minutes at 450 degrees F.
  2. Drain a can of tuna of its liquid.
  3. While the bread is toasting mix 2 table spoons of Mayonnaise with 1 can of tuna
  4. Add a generous amount of black pepper to the tuna.
  5. Add Swiss Cheese to the toasting bread slices and allow to melt for 1 more minute in the toaster oven.
  6. Remove the Bread from the toaster oven and place the tuna salad on the bottom slice.
  7. Cut a slice of tomato and romaine lettuce and place it on the tuna.
  8. Serve the Pickle Spear on the side of the sandwich.
  9. Feel free to serve with your favorite sides and drinks! Enjoy!
